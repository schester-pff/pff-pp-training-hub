PFFU E-Learning

🎓

What is PFFU?

PFFU is the PFF Enterprise Academy's online learning platform. Week 1 of your training is dedicated to working through the Player Participation process modules at your own pace. You must complete the course and pass all quizzes before you can access the Training DAT for your first game assignment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I move on from Week 1 to the paid training games?⌄
To move forward to Week 2, you must have watched all of the video material on PFFU and successfully completed the quizzes that accompany each section. Once you have done this, you will earn your certification and be cleared to move on to your first game assignment.
What happens if I do not pass or complete a training game assignment?⌄
Failure to complete a training game assignment by the deadline, or submitting an assignment where it is clear that little effort has been made to accurately input the correct data, will result in your removal from the Academy Program and you will forfeit your pay for that assignment.

Full details about the pay structure will be provided once you have completed the Week 1 assessment period.
When will I get paid for my training games?⌄
Pay is made in two separate payments via Deel. Those who complete Training Games 1 and 2 will be paid at the end of that fortnight. Those who complete Training Games 3 and 4 will be paid at the end of the following fortnight.

You will receive full details about pay and how to register with Deel once you have completed the Week 1 assessment period.
Do I need to be available at specific times or days during training?⌄
The only mandatory time commitment each week is attending two drop-in classroom sessions. We offer multiple sessions throughout the week so you can find two that work with your schedule.

In addition, we offer voluntary drop-in and one-to-one sessions each week. Attendance is not required, but they are strongly recommended for anyone serious about passing the course.
What equipment do I need?⌄
A PC or Mac is required — the PFF Enterprise data entry tool does not work on tablets or mobile. A reliable internet connection is also essential. A second monitor is not just recommended but a genuine necessity. If you don't have a spare monitor, connecting your laptop or PC to your TV via HDMI is a simple solution.
What do I need to do to pass the course and earn a contractor position?⌄
To earn a position for in-season work, you need to successfully complete all four training games to an acceptable standard. Your Game 4 assignment serves as your final assessment. Your performance across all four games determines whether you are offered a contractor position.

Full details about standards and passing grades will be shared once you have completed the Week 1 assessment period.
